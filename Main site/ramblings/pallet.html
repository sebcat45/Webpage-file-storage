The creepy music of Pallet Town

Even I don't like it!

Back

It's wild how unnerving the original music for pallet town was. People were literally getting nightmares from it, man!

Because of said complaints the big N changed it, just by lowering some notes. It went from creepy 8-bit horror music to normal town music.

If you watch any spooky video about pokemon it will most likely be using the original, no wonder you get an unsettling feeling when watching!
