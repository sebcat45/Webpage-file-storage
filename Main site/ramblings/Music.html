Lost Music

I'm pretty sure I have looped this for upwards of 1 hour

Back
I found this video about a lost song.
After watching it, youtube recommended the reupload of it. I put it on the Watch Later and forgot about it.
But youtube kept recommending it.
Eventually, at 1:00 AM, I opened it. And listened.
After a while I downloaded it, put it on loop, and began reading.
I was getting goosebumps, not being able to focus.
I shut down the wifi, data, location, sounds, and just sat.

Just sitting there, staring at the void, my headset's light pinging and my battery's dim glow, absorbed in this song, various thoughts floating in my skull.

the song looped. and looped. and looped.
the perfect thought floated past: write.
and so I did
even whilst writing this I was still getting goosebumps.
