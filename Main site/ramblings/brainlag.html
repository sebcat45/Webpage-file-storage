Brain Lag

I love how I can only think when I'm half asleep

Back

Weirdly, my brain works best when I am either standing in the shower, or typing at night. I guess because all of my other senses are filled with noise, therefore my subconscious filters them out, giving my brain more power to think.
after all, I did make most of this webpage at 1-4 am.

Meanwhile my brain's langauge and thought processing fight for power during the day, causing a
"*bla bla bla*in this you......uh.........will end up*bla bla*"
To happen as my brain scrables to both pull up the required info and talk at the same time

This can be especially bad if me and my team are in a "all hands on deck" sisituation in some online game like SoT and my brain decides to not talk. I am still firing the cannons, but I'm failing to do callouts.
